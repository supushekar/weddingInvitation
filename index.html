Our
Story

Our story began with an introduction by our families.

What started as a few conversations quickly turned into a friendship built on laughter, shared values, and a growing appreciation for one another. As we spent more time together, our connection deepened and naturally grew into something more.

We quickly discovered a shared love for coffee, hiking, travel, and long conversations that never seemed to end.

From Seattle to Vancouver, we’ve made memories exploring new places, crossing borders, and finding joy in both grand adventures and everyday moments. Our first trip together was to Niagara Falls, and it perhaps fitting that a place spanning two countries became a symbol of our own cross-border love story.

Through every adventure, conversation, and shared experience, we discovered not only a partner, but also a best friend in each other.

We are grateful to celebrate this next chapter with our family and friends.
